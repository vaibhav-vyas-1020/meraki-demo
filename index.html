Our Story

Crafted with soul.

At Meraki, we believe coffee is a feeling. Every cup is prepared with intentional care — from bean selection to the final pour. Our cozy, modern space in the heart of Surat invites you to slow down, connect, and savor the moment.

Whether you're here for a quick espresso or a long afternoon with friends, Meraki is your place. Casual enough for everyday, special enough for every occasion.
